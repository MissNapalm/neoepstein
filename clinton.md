# WHAT DOES BILL CLINTON KNOW?

### An Intelligence Assessment Based on Documented Network Analysis

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Between 1993 and 2003, the 42nd President of the United States maintained a sustained, documented relationship with Jeffrey Epstein — the most prolific child sex trafficker in modern American history. That relationship was not limited to social encounters. It was structural. Clinton's senior cabinet officials, his National Security Adviser, his Chief of Staff, his Vice President, and his closest foreign ally all appear independently in Epstein's contact files. Two of his senior officials were named under oath by a trafficking victim. His foundation accepted Epstein's money after Epstein's conviction. Ghislaine Maxwell attended his daughter's wedding two years after that conviction.

This report does not speculate about what Bill Clinton did on Jeffrey Epstein's island. It examines what the documented record — flight logs, sworn depositions, court filings, contact files, and public records — structurally requires him to have known.

## THE FLIGHTS

Bill Clinton flew on Jeffrey Epstein's private aircraft at least 26 times. This number is derived from publicly released flight logs and has not been meaningfully disputed by Clinton's representatives, who initially claimed "four trips" before the logs forced a revision.

Twenty-six flights on a private aircraft is not a social acquaintance. Commercial flights are incidental. Private aviation is intimate. The passengers are chosen. The destinations are known. The manifest is short. On a plane that seats fewer than twenty people, there is no crowd to disappear into.

On several of these flights, Clinton's Secret Service detail did not accompany him. This is the single most important fact in the flight record. The Secret Service exists to protect the President of the United States at all times. Their removal is not a logistical inconvenience. It is a deliberate act that requires the principal's explicit instruction. A former president does not accidentally leave behind his security detail. He orders them to stay.

The only reason to remove the Secret Service is to ensure there are no federal witnesses at the destination.

Clinton has never been asked under oath why he removed his protective detail. He has never been required to account for where he went on those flights or what he did when he arrived.

## THE CABINET

The contact files recovered from Epstein's residences do not merely contain Bill Clinton's name. They contain his government.

**Sandy Berger**, Clinton's National Security Adviser from 1997 to 2001, appears in Epstein's contact files. Berger later pleaded guilty in 2005 to deliberately removing and destroying classified documents from the National Archives — stuffing them into his socks and pants during visits meant to prepare his testimony for the 9/11 Commission. He was Clinton's most senior national security official. He destroyed classified records. He is in the contact files. These three facts exist simultaneously.

**Bill Richardson**, Clinton's Energy Secretary, was named under oath by Virginia Giuffre in federal depositions as a man she was directed to provide sexual services to. Richardson denied the allegations. He was never investigated. He was never deposed. He died in September 2023.

**George Mitchell**, Clinton's Special Envoy for Northern Ireland and later Middle East peace, was also named under oath by Giuffre. Mitchell denied the allegations. Like Richardson, he was never investigated or deposed.

**Donna Shalala**, Clinton's Secretary of Health and Human Services — the cabinet official with direct responsibility for federal child welfare policy — appears in Epstein's contact files. She served in that role from 1993 to 2001, the longest tenure in HHS history, spanning the entire period of Epstein's operational expansion. The person responsible for protecting American children from abuse had a documented connection to the man abusing them.

**Al Gore**, Clinton's Vice President, appears in the contact files.

**John Kerry**, who served in Clinton's broader political orbit and later as Secretary of State under Obama, appears in the contact files.

This is not one name in a contact book. This is a cabinet. The National Security Adviser, the Energy Secretary, the HHS Secretary, the Vice President, and a special envoy — all independently appearing in the files of a man who was running a child sex trafficking operation from properties in Manhattan, Palm Beach, New Mexico, the US Virgin Islands, and Paris.

## THE GATEKEEPER

John Podesta served as Bill Clinton's Chief of Staff from 1998 to 2001 — the precise years of peak documented Clinton-Epstein contact. He appears in Epstein's contact files.

The Chief of Staff controls the President's schedule. Every meeting, every trip, every flight is routed through that office. If Clinton flew on Epstein's plane 26 times, Podesta either approved those trips, arranged them, or was informed of them. There is no version of the White House scheduling process in which the President of the United States repeatedly boards a private aircraft without the Chief of Staff's knowledge.

Podesta knew where Clinton was going. He knew who he was travelling with. He knew how often it was happening. He is in the contact files independently.

## THE VICTIMS

Virginia Giuffre was recruited into Jeffrey Epstein's trafficking operation at age 16 while working as a locker room attendant at Donald Trump's Mar-a-Lago resort in Palm Beach. She was trafficked across state and international lines for years.

In sworn federal depositions, Giuffre named Bill Clinton as present on Epstein's private island in the US Virgin Islands. She named Richardson and Mitchell — both Clinton officials — as men she was directed to have sexual contact with.

Giuffre's testimony was given under oath, subject to the penalties of perjury. She maintained her account consistently across multiple depositions, media appearances, and legal proceedings spanning more than a decade. Her credibility was sufficient for Prince Andrew to pay an estimated £12 million to settle her civil claim rather than face her testimony in open court.

Clinton has never been deposed. He has never been required to testify under oath about his relationship with Epstein. No prosecutor has ever sought his testimony. No congressional committee has ever compelled it.

## THE WEDDING

On July 31, 2010, Chelsea Clinton married Marc Mezvinsky in Rhinebeck, New York. Among the guests was Ghislaine Maxwell.

Jeffrey Epstein had pleaded guilty in 2008 to procuring a minor for prostitution. By 2010, the nature of his operation was publicly known. Victim statements had been filed. The plea deal had been reported. Epstein was a registered sex offender.

Guest lists at a former President's daughter's wedding are not compiled casually. They are vetted by Secret Service. They are reviewed for security concerns. Every name is a deliberate invitation.

The Clintons invited Ghislaine Maxwell — Epstein's co-conspirator, recruiter, and the woman later convicted of sex trafficking — to their daughter's wedding, two years after Epstein's guilty plea made the operation public knowledge.

This is not a failure of due diligence. This is a choice. The Clintons chose loyalty to Maxwell over distance from a convicted sex trafficking operation. That choice was made with full knowledge of what Epstein had pleaded guilty to, and it was made in one of the most personally significant and carefully curated settings imaginable — their only daughter's wedding.

## THE FOUNDATION

The Clinton Foundation accepted donations from Jeffrey Epstein. The precise timeline of these donations has been disputed, but reporting has established that Epstein's financial support continued after his 2008 conviction.

Charitable foundations maintain donor vetting processes for exactly this reason. A convicted sex offender's donation to a former president's foundation should be the most flagged transaction in the compliance system. Either the foundation's vetting process identified Epstein as a convicted sex offender and accepted his money anyway, or the vetting process failed to identify the most high-profile sex offender in the country. Either answer implicates the institution.

## THE DESTRUCTION OF RECORDS

Sandy Berger's guilty plea for stealing and destroying classified documents from the National Archives deserves separate consideration in this context.

Berger did not accidentally mishandle paperwork. He deliberately concealed classified documents in his clothing during multiple visits to the Archives, removed them from the building, and destroyed some of them. The documents related to the Clinton administration's counterterrorism record and were being reviewed in preparation for the 9/11 Commission.

He was Clinton's National Security Adviser. He committed federal crimes to control what the historical record showed about the Clinton administration. He is in Epstein's contact files.

The question is not whether Berger's document destruction was related to Epstein — there is no direct evidence of that specific connection. The question is what it reveals about the Clinton administration's institutional relationship to accountability. When the historical record threatened to reveal uncomfortable truths, Clinton's most senior national security official committed a crime to alter it. That is the culture. That is the apparatus. And that apparatus is in Epstein's contact files.

## ASSESSMENT

The network analysis does not support the conclusion that Bill Clinton was an unwitting social acquaintance of Jeffrey Epstein who was caught up in something he didn't understand.

Twenty-six flights. Ditched Secret Service. A cabinet in the files. Officials named by victims. Destroyed records. Post-conviction foundation donations. Maxwell at the wedding.

The structure of the documented record requires, at minimum, that Clinton knew who Epstein was, knew what Epstein did, knew that his own senior officials were involved, and chose to maintain the relationship — publicly, financially, and socially — after the conviction made the nature of the operation a matter of public record.

The question has never been what Bill Clinton knew.

The question is why no one with a subpoena has ever been required to ask him.
